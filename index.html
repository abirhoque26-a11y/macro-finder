How many different recipes to rotate through across the week for each meal. Set to 1 for the same one every day, or higher for more variety. Defaults show your own example: same breakfast, a few lunches and dinners on rotation.

Prices are rough per-serving estimates for UK supermarkets and vary by store and season. Calories and protein are set per recipe; carbs and fat are estimated from the remaining calories and the recipe's main carb source, so treat them as a close guide rather than a lab measurement. Where a matching video exists, "Watch video" links straight to it; where it doesn't, "View recipe" always gives full written instructions regardless. Ingredient links and the shopping list open live searches on Tesco, Sainsbury's, Asda, Aldi and Lidl. In a deficit, matches lean toward higher protein-per-calorie recipes; in a surplus, toward more energy-dense ones — both still ranked primarily by closeness to your calorie and protein targets. On accounts: sign-in uses real email/password authentication via Supabase — your favourites, cooking checklist and saved plans are stored in a real database under your account, and Row Level Security policies mean only you can read or write your own rows. Recipe photos are illustrative food photography, not photos of the exact finished dish.
