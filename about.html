We built Macro Mastery because planning meals that actually hit your goals — while staying within budget, fitting your diet, and not eating the same thing every day — was harder than it needed to be.

What we do

Macro Mastery is a weekly meal planner built around your macros, not the other way round. Tell us your calorie and protein targets, your dietary requirements, what you like to eat and what you can spend — and we'll build your week, generate a consolidated shopping list, and give you full recipes with instructions.

Every recipe comes with meal-prep guidance, store links for Tesco, Sainsbury's, Asda, Aldi and Lidl, and a video where one's available. The weekly plan tells you exactly what to cook, what to buy and how much it'll cost.

What makes us different

01

Built around your budget

Set a weekly budget and the planner stays within it. If you're over, it tells you exactly which meals to swap and how much you'll save — not just that you're over.

02

Actually personalised

Deficit, maintenance or surplus. Vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free. Beginner or advanced cook. Chicken or tofu. Rice or pasta. The plan adapts to your life, not a generic template.

03

82+ recipes and growing

We add new recipes every month across all meal types — breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks and shakes — with full written instructions and meal-prep tips on every single one.

04

Portion scaling

Recipes automatically scale to your calorie target, so whether you're eating 1600 kcal or 3500 kcal, the portions and macros adjust rather than showing you a fixed recipe that doesn't fit.
