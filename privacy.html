Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it's stored and protected. If you have any questions, email us at macromastery@outlook.com.

Who we are

Macro Mastery is a meal planning web application available at macromastery.uk, operated by the Macro Mastery team.

What data we collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used to identify your account and for sign-in.
  • Password — stored as a secure hash. We never store or see your actual password.

When you use the app, we store:

  • Saved meal plans — the weekly plans you choose to save.
  • Favourite recipes — recipes you've hearted.
  • Cooking checklist progress — which days you've ticked off in the weekly plan.

We do not collect your name, address, phone number, payment details, or any health or dietary information beyond what you voluntarily enter into the app (which is stored only in your browser session, not on our servers).

How your data is stored

All account data is stored in a secure database provided by Supabase (supabase.com), hosted in the EU (Frankfurt region). Supabase uses Row Level Security — a database-level security policy that ensures your data can only be accessed by you. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

How we use your data

We use your data only to:

  • Identify you when you sign in
  • Save and restore your meal plans, favourites and checklist progress across sessions

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising. We do not share your data with any third parties except Supabase, which processes it on our behalf as described above.

Cookies and tracking

We use a session cookie set by Supabase to keep you signed in. This is a strictly necessary cookie — the app cannot function without it. We do not currently use any analytics cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tracking.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at macromastery@outlook.com and we'll respond within 30 days.

Data retention

We keep your account data for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your account and all associated data within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests: macromastery@outlook.com
